Welcome to MLAB Sapporo 2012

What it's about:

2012 Sapporo Workshop on Machine Learning and Applications to Biology (MLAB Sapporo 2012) is a two-day event that will bring people together to share and discuss the latest advances, exchange ideas, and encourage collaboration in machine learning and biological data analytics. About 20 leading researchers will give talks at the workshop that will cover broad spectrum. This opportunity will also allow the participants to get in touch with experts in this field.

Who should attend:

The workshop is open to all for no charge (only the banquet will be charged). It is intended for anyone interested in the talks to be presented (including all academic/corporate reseachers, IT professionals, undergrad/grad students, staff, and faculty).

All participants are required to register in advance. Register NOW and be sure to share this with friends and colleagues who may be interested in this topic. Space is limited. Send this form (form.txt) by email to

to register. If you wish to attend the banquet, please also indicate it on the form.

When and where:

The workshop will be held from August 6-7, 2012 on the campus of Hokkaido University in Sapporo, Hokkaido.
